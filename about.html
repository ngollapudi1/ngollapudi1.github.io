We are the Best

Lobsters scavenge for food for optimal chance of survival, we can interpret their behavior as a sign that we need to prowl through our emotional landscape. We need to search for ambitions, instincts and thoughts that we may have allowed to wither away or be suppressed.The hard exterior of a lobster animal totem acts as a protective sheath and is symbolic of the guard that we put up in order to protect ourselves. Protection is a key theme of the lobster spirit guide. It reminds us that we must be in charge of looking after ourselves. We must take care of our heart and mind, in addition to nurturing and protecting our physical bodies. In addition to safeguarding, the lobster totem symbolizes strength of character in the form of resolution, self-control, nobility, abundance, and regeneration.
